The song "Strongest" was released on October 27, 2017, and peaked at number 2 in Norway. It marks Ina Wroldsen's first solo release under Syco Music, following her signing with the label in June 2017. The track centers its message around the heartbreak of a lost love and moving forward for her son.

In an interview with Charlotte Sissener from Musikknyheter, Wroldsen stated that "Strongest" is about a good friend of hers, describing the situation as "hard and bitter but also genuine and triumphant."
